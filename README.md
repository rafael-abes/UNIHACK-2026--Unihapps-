# UNIHACK-2026-UNIHAPPS- Spontaneous Social Coordination 

Happs is a mobile app designed to help students coordinate spontaneous activities on campus. The goal is to make it easy to see who is around, create quick events ("happs"), and invite friends or nearby students to join activities like food runs, sports, study sessions, or campus events.

The app uses location awareness, schedule information, and user status to intelligently notify users about activities they can realistically attend.

# Problem

University students often struggle to coordinate spontaneous social activities because:
- People don’t know who is currently on campus.

- Messaging groups are noisy and inefficient.

- It is difficult to quickly organise small activities like grabbing food or playing badminton.

- Schedules make availability unclear.

Happs solves this by combining location awareness, schedule context, and lightweight event creation to enable quick social coordination.
